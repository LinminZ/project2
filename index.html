The story follows the transient lives of four people at the margins of Chinese society. An anonymous videographer opens the film with a monologue on the eponymous Suzhou River and love. As the film's narra tor, the videographer is nevertheless never seen aside from his hands, as the camera serves as his first-person perspective for the audience.
